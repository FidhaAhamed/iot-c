MACE IoT Club

About

IoT club is a student community in MACE where students can learn, explore and improve their practical skills in the field of Internet of Things.

MACE IoT Club

The Internet of Things (IoT) has become increasingly important in the current situation, as it enables the connection and communication between devices, systems, and people through the internet. IoT technology has been used in various industries and applications, such as healthcare, agriculture, transportation, and manufacturing, among others.

IoT has become an essential technology in the current situation and will continue to play a critical role in shaping the future of various industries and applications. Being aware of the latest trends and demands in the industry is crucial for engineering students. By joining this club students can benefit from a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for IoT

MACE IoT Club has an Execom of 20 members. Main aim of the execom is to help the students to gain knowledge in the field of IoT by conducting workshops , hands-on sessions , talk sessions by professionals etc. We have an IoT lab in the college so that the members can lend components and can do projects. These projects are mentored by the execom members.
